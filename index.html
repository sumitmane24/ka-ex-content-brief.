Follow these guidelines to create compliant, high-converting content

✓
DO'S
✓Show the mixing process — powder-to-drink ASMR style performs very well on TikTok
✓Share your personal wellness journey and how KA-EX fits authentically into YOUR routine
✓Cite clinical data accurately: "up to 36% cortisol reduction in studies" with disclaimer
✓Show product clearly on camera — full label visible for at least 3 seconds
✓Include a clear #ad or #sponsored disclosure per FTC guidelines in caption and on-screen
✓Use #TikTokShop and shop link in caption with a clear CTA
✓Feature the 3-Pack bundle to highlight value — show all three pouches on camera
✓Include on-screen text with key stats (36%, +16% REM, etc.) for scroll-stopping power
✓Mention real athlete endorsements (Lara Gut, Fabian Cancellara) to build credibility
✗
DON'TS
✗Never claim KA-EX "cures," "treats," or "prevents" any disease or medical condition
✗Don't exaggerate results — say "helped me" or "in studies" rather than absolute guarantees
✗Avoid comparing KA-EX negatively to competitor products by name
✗Never create fake before/after transformations or fabricate testimonials
✗Don't skip the #ad / #paid disclosure — FTC compliance is required
✗Avoid targeting content at users under 18 — KA-EX is for adults only
✗Don't post without Pattern approval if content includes modified health claims
✗Never state KA-EX replaces doctor-prescribed medication or mental health treatment
✗Don't edit out the FDA disclaimer if displaying clinical statistics on screen
