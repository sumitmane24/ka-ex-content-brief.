04 Compliance & Content

Do’s & Don’ts.

The fast-track to high-converting, compliant content. FTC + FDA aligned — mandatory on every post.

+ Do this.

  • Show powder mixing / push-cap ASMR — a high-performing format on TikTok.
  • Use the “No Ashwagandha” angle — frame as “it works, just too slow.”
  • Show KA-EX on rest days — cortisol stays elevated 48–72h post-workout.
  • Cite stats: “up to −36% in peer-reviewed studies” + FDA disclaimer.
  • Show HRV / sleep app screenshots — personal data = TikTok credibility.
  • Use a matter-of-fact tone — less selling = more conversions (Round 1 proven).
  • Include #ad / #sponsored disclosure in caption and on-screen (FTC).
  • Mention Tudor Pro Cycling, Lara Gut-Behrami for instant credibility.
  • Reference “25 ingredients,” “no caffeine, no stimulants.”
  • Name “cortisol” early — audience recognises the problem before the solution.
  • Use specific numbers on-screen: −36%, 48h, 72h — beats general claims.

× Avoid this.

  • Never claim KA-EX “cures,” “treats,” or “prevents” any disease.
  • Don’t attack Ashwagandha brands by name — science comparison only.
  • Don’t guarantee results — “up to 36% in studies,” never absolute claims.
  • No fake before/afters or fabricated testimonials.
  • Don’t skip #ad / #paid — FTC compliance is mandatory on every post.
  • Don’t target content at under-18s.
  • Don’t post modified health claims without Pattern approval first.
  • Never say KA-EX replaces medication or mental health treatment.
  • Avoid excited testimonial tone — matter-of-fact outperforms.
  • Don’t only show post-workout — rest day content = missed opportunity.
  • Don’t remove the FDA disclaimer when using clinical statistics on-screen.

06 Legal & Compliance

Stay compliant.

All health claims must be truthful, non-misleading, and supported by KA-EX’s peer-reviewed clinical data.

★ FDA Disclaimer — Required When Citing Clinical Statistics

These statements have not been evaluated by the Food and Drug Administration. KA-EX® Cortisol Stress Reducer is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Individual results may vary. Consult your healthcare provider before use.

Health Claims

All claims must be truthful and not misleading. Stick to cleared language: “up to −36% in peer-reviewed studies.” Avoid absolute or guaranteed outcomes.

No Disease Claims

KA-EX is a dietary supplement, not a medication. Never imply it cures, treats, or prevents anxiety, depression, insomnia, or any clinical condition.

Before/After Imagery

No fake or implied before/after transformations. No fabricated testimonials. Personal experience must be authentic and clearly framed as individual results.

FTC Disclosure

Every TikTok post must include #ad or #sponsored in the caption and as visible on-screen text. Disclosure must be clear, conspicuous, and placed early.

Mental Health

Never position KA-EX as a substitute for medication, therapy, or professional mental health treatment. Cortisol management is not mental health treatment.

Approval Required

Any modification to cleared claims, new health angles, or unscripted product descriptions must be reviewed by Pattern Commerce before publishing.
